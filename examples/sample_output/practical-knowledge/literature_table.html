Practical knowledge literature — engagement table

Papers turned up by the citation crawl, scored 0–3 for depth of engagement on each issue. Leverage = weighted depth across the six core issues (A1–A4 weight 1, A5–A6 weight 0.5; max 15). Showing the higher-leverage set by default; lighter-engagement papers are hidden until you ask for them. ★ marks priority papers: either leverage ≥ with solid in-corpus uptake, or a heavily-cited-within-this-corpus work of somewhat lower leverage (or a hand-kept founding text). Reviews are never starred. Rows tagged no abstract are topically relevant works for which no abstract could be found; they are admitted on relevance + in-corpus citation count, their leverage shows as -- (not scored from a title alone), and they sort by citedness.

Case-insensitive. Operators must be UPPER-CASE. A term with no field searches author + title + abstract. If a query won't parse, it falls back to a plain substring match.
